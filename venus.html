🪐 Venus: Earth's Mysterious Twin

~ 🔍 General Information:

-Position from the Sun: 2nd planet

-Orbital Period: Approximately 225 Earth days

-Rotation Period: About 243 Earth days (retrograde rotation)

-Diameter: Approximately 12,104 km

-Moons: None​

~ 🌡️ Surface and Climate:

-Temperature: Average surface temperature around 465°C (869°F), making it the hottest planet in our solar system

-Atmosphere: Dense atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid

-Surface Features: Volcanic plains, vast highland regions, and numerous volcanoes; surface pressure about 92 times that of Earth

~ 🧲 Internal Structure:

-Core: Likely consists of a metallic core, similar to Earth's

-Magnetic Field: Lacks a significant intrinsic magnetic field
