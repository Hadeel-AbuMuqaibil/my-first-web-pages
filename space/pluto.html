🪐 Pluto: The Dwarf Planet of the Kuiper Belt

~ 🔍 General Information: 

-Classification: Dwarf planet​

-Location: Kuiper Belt, beyond Neptune​

-Discovery: 1930 by Clyde Tombaugh​

-Diameter: Approximately 2,377 km (1,477 miles)​

-Orbital Period: About 248 Earth years​

-Day Length: Approximately 6.4 Earth days​

-Moons: 5 known moons; the largest is Charon

~ 🌫️ Surface and Atmosphere: 

Surface Features: Mountains, valleys, plains, craters, and glaciers​

Notable Region: Tombaugh Regio, a heart-shaped area with diverse terrain​

Atmosphere: Thin atmosphere composed mainly of nitrogen, with traces of methane and carbon monoxide
