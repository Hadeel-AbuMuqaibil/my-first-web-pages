~ 🌍 Earth: Our Home in the Solar System

~ 🌐 General Information:

-Position from the Sun: 3rd planet​

-Orbital Period: Approximately 365.25 days​

-Rotation Period: Approximately 24 hours​

-Diameter: Approximately 12,742 km​

-Moons: 1 (the Moon)​

~ 🌊 Surface and Atmosphere:


-Surface Composition: Approximately 70.8% water and 29.2% land​

-Atmosphere: Composed mainly of nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%), with traces of other gases​

-Average Surface Temperature: Approximately 14.76°C

~ 🧲 Internal Structure: 

-Core: Consists of a solid inner core and a liquid outer core, generating Earth's magnetic field​

-Mantle and Crust: Composed of silicate rocks, with tectonic activity shaping the surface

~ 🌱 Life and Biodiversity: 

-Earth is the only known planet to support life, with a vast diversity of ecosystems and species​

-The biosphere interacts with the atmosphere, hydrosphere, and geosphere to sustain life
