🪐 Uranus: The Tilted Ice Giant

~ 🔍 General Information: 

-Position from the Sun: 7th planet

-Diameter: Approximately 50,724 km

-Length of Day: About 17 hours and 14 minutes

-Orbital Period: Approximately 84 Earth years

-Moons: 28 known moons, including Titania and Oberon

-Rings: 13 faint rings composed of dark particles

~ 🌫️ Atmosphere and Composition: 

-Atmosphere: Primarily hydrogen and helium, with methane

-Color: The methane gives Uranus its blue-green hue

-Composition: An ice giant with a small rocky core surrounded by water, ammonia, and methane ices

~ 🌀 Unique Features: 

-Axial Tilt: Approximately 98 degrees, causing it to rotate nearly on its side

-Seasons: Extreme seasonal variations due to its tilt

-Magnetic Field: Unusual orientation and structure, differing from other planets
