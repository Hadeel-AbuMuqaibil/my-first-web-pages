🪐 Mars: The Red Planet

~ 🔍 General Information: 

-Position from the Sun: 4th planet

-Diameter: About 6,779 km

-Length of Day: Approximately 24.6 Earth hours

-Orbital Period: About 687 Earth days

-Moons: 2 (Phobos and Deimos)

~ 🌋 Surface Features: 

-Color: Reddish appearance due to iron oxide (rust) on the surface

-Olympus Mons: Tallest volcano in the solar system

-Valles Marineris: A massive canyon system stretching over 4,000 km

-Poles: Covered with ice made of water and carbon dioxide

~ 🌫️ Atmosphere and Climate: 

-Atmosphere: Thin, composed mostly of carbon dioxide

-Air Pressure: Less than 1% of Earth’s atmospheric pressure

-Temperature: Ranges from -125°C at night to 20°C during the day

-Weather: Dust storms can cover the entire planet

~ 🧲 Internal Structure: 

-Core: Likely a mix of iron, nickel, and sulfur, not fully solid

-Crust: Thicker than Earth's, made mostly of basaltic rock
