🪐 Mercury: The Swift Planet

~ 🔍 General Information:

-Position from the Sun: 1st planet

-Orbital Period: 88 Earth days

-Rotation Period: Approximately 59 Earth days

-Diameter: About 4,880 km

-Moons: None​

~ 🌡️ Surface and Climate:

-Temperature Range: From -173°C at night to 427°C during the day

-Atmosphere: Extremely thin exosphere composed mainly of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium

-Surface Features: Heavily cratered terrain, similar to Earth's Moon, with vast plains and cliffs

~ 🧲 Internal Structure:

-Core: Large metallic core making up about 85% of the planet's radius

-Magnetic Field: Present, but only about 1% as strong as Earth's​
